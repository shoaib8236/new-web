Children Support

Get the most out of your support services

At Coastal Community Support Services, we offer a range of support services for children with a variety of needs.

Our Services include:
  • Support for high behaviour needs
  • Cleaning
  • Cooking
  • Allied health appointments
  • School & kindergarten transport
  • School holiday activities
  • Staff clearances for working with children
  • Respite

Families can also receive emotional support and guidance from our team, which can be incredibly helpful during difficult times. If you need assistance with child support services, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We want to make your life as easy as possible, so you can focus on what's important.

Now Offering Water Sports With Tom!

Tom is our newest team member and he is a lifesaver! With extensive knowledge and experience around water, surfing and children, Tom has the skills and passion to help keep your little ones safe. He has taught at Vacswim & Surf Babies, is a current Surf Life Saver at Moana SLSC, and holds a current First Aid & CPR certificate. So if you're looking for someone who can provide unique support that will have your children loving the water, Tom is your man! Plus, all of our equipment is provided - so you don't have to worry about a thing.
