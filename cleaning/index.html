About Karen

Karen has over 30 years of experience in the domestic and industrial cleaning industry. With 10 years of experience in nursing homes, she has knowledge and understanding of how to clean areas with specific needs. Karen is familiar and has knowledge in Aged Care, Parkinson’s, Dementia, ASD, ADHD and more. Karen also supplies cleaning equipment and products to help make the cleaning process easier for her clients.
