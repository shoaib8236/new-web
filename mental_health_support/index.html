Get the most out of your support services

Coastal Community Support can play an important role in supporting mental health through a variety of initiatives and services.

Our Services include:
  • Core Support
  • Appointment Assistance
  • Daily Living
  • Home & Garden Maintenance
  • Food Preparation and Cooking
  • Cleaning
  • Psychosocial
  • Drug & Alcohol Dependency Support
  • Parole Appointments
  • Respite

In particular, we can provide a sense of connectedness and belonging, which is often crucial for those struggling with mental illness. The support of others can make a big difference in someone's ability to cope with mental illness and maintain their mental health.

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental illness, there are a number of services and support groups available. These can provide practical assistance and emotional support, as well as help connect you with other people in similar situations.
